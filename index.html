My Services

CONSULTATION & PROPOSAL

We'll kick things off by having a conversation about your business or organization, covering the history of your project and your goals for the website. We'll pinpoint your target audience, analyze your competitors, discuss design preferences, and identify key features. With a solid understanding of your vision, I'll put together a detailed project proposal and contract.

CREATION

Websites are a visual medium, so I'll start by creating several mockups based on our consultation for you to choose from. Once we've nailed down the design, I'll begin constructing the website's structure while you gather the necessary content—text, images, logos, etc. After you provide your content, I'll integrate it into the site and give you time to review and suggest any changes. When the final updates are complete, I'll launch your website.

LAUNCH & SUPPORT

Once your website is live, I'll keep an eye on its performance and offer Search Engine Optimization recommendations based on early web traffic. I enjoy long-term collaborations, so if you ever need updates, changes, or new features in the future, I'm happy to help maintain your website so it remains effective and engaging.
